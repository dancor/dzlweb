Offer Conditions

This pre-approval does not represent an obligation or commitment by Block Financial Resources LLC and/or its Lenders to make the requested loan to the applicant.
A mortgage loan commitment can only be supplied to the applicant when Block Financial Resources LLC and/or its Lenders have both received and reviewed: 1) A completed and signed Uniform Residential Loan Application (1003) 2) an acceptable appraisal of the subject property 3) an acceptable credit report for all mortgage borrowers and/or guarantors 4) supplementary underwriting documents as requested by Block Financial Resources LLC and/or its Lenders.
Please be advised that mortgage interest rates are subject to change without prior notice; interest rates may only be locked after the receipt and examination of the documents listed above.
The fees listed for this approval are not inclusive of any city or state fees, hazard, flood or title insurance fees or any other closing and settlement fees as may be required.
Block Financial Resources LLC is not accountable for typographical errors, or any problems that transpire during the transmission of data.

**Estimated monthly payment for non-fixed interest rate products is for the initial payment only. Non-fixed interests rate products have adjustable rates and terms and subsequent monthly payments will adjust higher or lower.
